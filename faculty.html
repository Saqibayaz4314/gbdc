Message from the Principal

Welcome to Government Boys Degree College Jacobabad. Our institution is committed to providing quality education and fostering academic excellence. We have a team of highly qualified and dedicated faculty members who are passionate about teaching and mentoring students.

Our faculty members not only impart knowledge but also instill values that help students become responsible citizens. They are constantly updating their skills and knowledge to keep pace with the changing educational landscape.

I am proud to lead such a distinguished team of educators who are making a significant contribution to the development of our students and society at large.

Prof. Ayaz Ahmed Soomro

Principal, GBDC Jacobabad

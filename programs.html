Academic Programs

Government Boys Degree College Jacobabad offers a wide range of undergraduate programs designed to provide students with comprehensive knowledge and skills in various fields. Our programs are aligned with the requirements of the Higher Education Commission (HEC) and are designed to meet the needs of the job market.

We offer programs in Science, Arts, Commerce, and Information Technology. Each program is designed to provide students with theoretical knowledge as well as practical skills through laboratory work, projects, and internships.

Bachelor of Science (BS)

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science (BS) program at GBDC Jacobabad is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in scientific principles and their applications. The program emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical skills through extensive laboratory work.

Specializations Offered:

  • BS in Physics Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)

    The BS Physics program covers classical mechanics, electromagnetism, quantum mechanics, thermodynamics, and modern physics.

  • BS in Chemistry Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)

    The BS Chemistry program includes organic chemistry, inorganic chemistry, physical chemistry, analytical chemistry, and biochemistry.

  • BS in Mathematics Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)

    The BS Mathematics program covers calculus, differential equations, linear algebra, abstract algebra, and numerical analysis.

  • BS in Botany Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)

    The BS Botany program includes plant anatomy, plant physiology, plant taxonomy, ecology, and genetics.

  • BS in Zoology Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)

    The BS Zoology program covers animal diversity, animal physiology, genetics, evolution, and ecology.

Career Opportunities:

Graduates of our BS programs can pursue careers in research, education, healthcare, environmental management, and various industries. They can also pursue higher studies such as MS or PhD in their respective fields.

Bachelor of Arts (BA)

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Arts (BA) program at GBDC Jacobabad offers a comprehensive education in humanities and social sciences. The program aims to develop critical thinking, communication skills, and a deep understanding of human society and culture.

Specializations Offered:

  • BA in Sindhi Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)

    The BA Sindhi program covers Sindhi literature, poetry, prose, linguistics, and cultural studies.

  • BA in Urdu Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)

    The BA Urdu program includes Urdu literature, poetry, prose, criticism, and linguistics.

  • BA in English Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)

    The BA English program covers English literature, language, linguistics, and communication skills.

  • BA in Economics Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)

    The BA Economics program includes microeconomics, macroeconomics, development economics, and economic policies.

  • BA in Political Science Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)

    The BA Political Science program covers political theory, comparative politics, international relations, and public administration.

Career Opportunities:

Graduates of our BA programs can pursue careers in education, journalism, civil service, politics, NGOs, and international organizations. They can also pursue higher studies such as MA or MPhil in their respective fields.

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) program at GBDC Jacobabad is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in business and commerce. The program emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical skills through case studies and projects.

Program Details:

  • B.Com (Regular) Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)

    The B.Com program covers accounting, finance, business law, economics, taxation, and business management.

  • B.Com (Honors) Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)

    The B.Com Honors program provides an in-depth study of accounting, finance, business law, economics, taxation, and business management with specialized courses and a research project.

Career Opportunities:

Graduates of our B.Com programs can pursue careers in accounting, banking, finance, taxation, auditing, and business management. They can also pursue professional certifications such as CA, ACCA, or CMA, or higher studies such as MBA or MS in Finance.

Information Technology

Program Overview

The Information Technology program at GBDC Jacobabad is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science and information technology. The program emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical skills through extensive lab work and projects.

Program Details:

  • BS in Computer Science Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)

    The BS Computer Science program covers programming, data structures, algorithms, database systems, operating systems, software engineering, and web development.

  • BS in Information Technology Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)

    The BS Information Technology program includes networking, system administration, cybersecurity, web technologies, and IT project management.

  • Associate Degree in Information Technology Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)

    The Associate Degree program provides fundamental knowledge and skills in computer applications, web development, and basic programming.

Career Opportunities:

Graduates of our IT programs can pursue careers as software developers, web developers, system administrators, network engineers, database administrators, cybersecurity specialists, and IT project managers. They can also pursue higher studies such as MS or PhD in Computer Science or related fields.

Admission Requirements

Eligibility Criteria

  • For BS/B.Com (Honors) Programs: Intermediate (HSSC) or equivalent with minimum 50% marks
  • For BA/B.Com Programs: Intermediate (HSSC) or equivalent with minimum 45% marks
  • For Associate Degree: Intermediate (HSSC) or equivalent with minimum 45% marks

Required Documents

  • Intermediate (HSSC) Certificate and Mark Sheet
  • Matriculation (SSC) Certificate and Mark Sheet
  • Character Certificate from the last institution attended
  • Copy of CNIC or B-Form
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Domicile Certificate

Admission Schedule

  • Admission Advertisement: July/August (Fall) and December/January (Spring)
  • Form Submission: Within 2 weeks of advertisement
  • Merit List Display: Within 1 week of form submission deadline
  • Fee Submission: Within 1 week of merit list display
  • Classes Commencement: As per academic calendar

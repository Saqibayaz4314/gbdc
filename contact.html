Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements for the college?

Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, students need to have completed their intermediate/higher secondary education with a minimum of 45% marks. For more specific requirements, please visit our Admission page or contact the admission office.

What programs does the college offer?

The college offers a variety of undergraduate programs in Science, Arts, and Commerce, including Bachelor of Science (BS), Bachelor of Arts (BA), and Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com). For a complete list of programs, please visit our Programs page.

Is there any hostel facility available for students?

Yes, the college provides hostel facilities for students who come from distant areas. The hostel has all the necessary amenities to ensure a comfortable stay for students. For more information, please contact the hostel warden.

Are there any scholarships available for students?

Yes, the college offers various scholarships based on merit, need, and other criteria. The scholarships are provided by the Government of Sindh, Higher Education Commission, and other organizations. For more information, please contact the scholarship cell.

How can I apply for admission to the college?

You can apply for admission by filling out the admission form available on our website or by visiting the admission office. The admission process is usually conducted once a year, after the announcement of intermediate/higher secondary results.

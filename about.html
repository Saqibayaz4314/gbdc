Our History

Government Boys Degree College Jacobabad was established in 1965 with the aim of providing quality higher education to the youth of Jacobabad and surrounding areas. Since its inception, the college has grown tremendously in terms of infrastructure, academic programs, and student enrollment.

Over the years, the college has produced thousands of graduates who have gone on to excel in various fields including medicine, engineering, law, education, and civil service. The college takes pride in its contribution to the socio-economic development of the region through quality education.

The college campus was initially located in a small building with limited facilities. However, with the support of the Government of Sindh, the campus was expanded, and new buildings were constructed to accommodate the growing number of students and to provide modern educational facilities.

Our Journey

1965

Establishment

Government Boys Degree College Jacobabad was established to provide quality education to the youth of the region.

1975

Introduction of Science Programs

The college expanded its academic offerings by introducing Bachelor of Science programs in various disciplines.

1985

Campus Expansion

The college campus was expanded with new buildings, laboratories, and a library to accommodate the growing number of students.

1995

Computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department was established to provide education in emerging technology fields.

2005

Modern Facilities

The college upgraded its infrastructure with modern facilities including digital classrooms, computer labs, and sports facilities.

2015

Golden Jubilee

The college celebrated its 50th anniversary with various academic and cultural events.

Present

Continued Excellence

The college continues to provide quality education and produce graduates who excel in various fields.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for their personal and professional growth, and to contribute to the socio-economic development of the region through academic excellence and community service.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Sindh, recognized for its academic excellence, innovative teaching methodologies, research contributions, and commitment to producing graduates who are competent, ethical, and socially responsible.

Our Values

  • Excellence in education
  • Integrity and ethics
  • Respect for diversity
  • Community service
  • Innovation and creativity

Principal's Message

Welcome to Government Boys Degree College Jacobabad, an institution committed to providing quality education and nurturing the talents of young minds. Our college has a rich history of academic excellence, and we take pride in our contribution to the educational landscape of Jacobabad.

At GBDC Jacobabad, we believe in holistic education that goes beyond textbooks and classrooms. We focus on the overall development of our students, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to excel in their chosen fields and to become responsible citizens.

Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and diverse academic programs create an environment conducive to learning and growth. We encourage our students to explore their potential, pursue their passions, and strive for excellence in all their endeavors.

As we move forward, we remain committed to our mission of providing quality education and to our vision of being a leading educational institution in Sindh. We invite you to join us on this journey of academic excellence and personal growth.

Prof. Ayaz Ahmed Soomro

Principal, GBDC Jacobabad

Our Facilities

Library

Our well-stocked library houses over 20,000 books, journals, and reference materials to support academic and research activities.

Science Labs

State-of-the-art laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and other science subjects with modern equipment and facilities.

Computer Labs

Modern computer laboratories with high-speed internet connectivity to facilitate IT education and research.

Classrooms

Spacious and well-ventilated classrooms equipped with modern teaching aids to enhance the learning experience.

Sports Facilities

Extensive sports facilities including cricket ground, football field, basketball court, and indoor games facilities.

Mosque

A beautiful mosque on campus for prayers and religious activities, promoting spiritual growth alongside academic excellence.

Auditorium

A modern auditorium for hosting academic, cultural, and other events, with a seating capacity of 500 people.

WiFi Campus

The entire campus is equipped with high-speed WiFi connectivity to facilitate learning and research.

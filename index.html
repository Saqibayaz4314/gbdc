Welcome to GBDC Jacobabad

Shaping Future Leaders

Government Boys Degree College Jacobabad is a premier educational institution committed to providing quality education to the youth of Jacobabad and surrounding areas. Established in 1965, our college has been a center of academic excellence for over five decades.

We offer a diverse range of undergraduate programs in Science, Arts, and Commerce, providing students with comprehensive knowledge and skills necessary for their future careers.

University Affiliations

Shah Abdul Latif University, Khairpur Mir's

Government Boys Degree College Jacobabad is proudly affiliated with Shah Abdul Latif University, Khairpur Mir's, one of the most prestigious universities in Sindh Province.

This affiliation ensures that our academic programs meet the highest standards of quality education and our degrees are recognized nationally and internationally.

Through this partnership, our students benefit from standardized curriculum, quality assurance measures, and opportunities for higher education at SALU.

Visit University Website

Shaikh Ayaz University, Shikarpur

We are also affiliated with Shaikh Ayaz University, Shikarpur, which has emerged as a center of academic excellence in the region.

This dual affiliation provides our students with broader academic exposure and access to the resources and expertise of two leading universities in Sindh.

The affiliation with Shaikh Ayaz University especially strengthens our programs in arts, humanities and social sciences, offering students expanded opportunities for research and career advancement.

Admission Process

Government Boys Degree College Jacobabad welcomes applications from qualified students for our various undergraduate programs. The admission process is merit-based and transparent, ensuring equal opportunity for all eligible candidates.

1

Application Submission

Download the application form from our website or collect it from the college admission office. Fill it out completely and submit it along with the required documents before the deadline.

2

Document Verification

All submitted documents will be verified by the admission committee. Incomplete applications or those with false information will be rejected.

3

Merit List Preparation

Based on the academic records and as per the admission criteria, merit lists will be prepared and displayed on the college notice board and website.

4

Fee Submission

Selected candidates must submit the required fees within the specified timeframe to secure their admission.

5

Orientation

Newly admitted students will attend an orientation session to familiarize themselves with the college facilities, rules, and regulations.

Eligibility Criteria

Bachelor of Science (BS)

  • Intermediate (HSSC) with Pre-Medical or Pre-Engineering with minimum 50% marks
  • For BS Computer Science, Intermediate with Computer Science or Mathematics is accepted
  • Age limit: 24 years (relaxable in special cases)

Bachelor of Arts (BA)

  • Intermediate (HSSC) in any discipline with minimum 45% marks
  • For specific subjects, relevant background may be preferred
  • Age limit: 24 years (relaxable in special cases)

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

  • Intermediate (HSSC) with Commerce or Arts with Economics with minimum 45% marks
  • For B.Com (Honors), minimum 50% marks are required
  • Age limit: 24 years (relaxable in special cases)

Information Technology

  • Intermediate (HSSC) with Computer Science, Mathematics, or Physics with minimum 50% marks
  • For Associate Degree, minimum 45% marks are required
  • Age limit: 24 years (relaxable in special cases)

Required Documents

  • Completed Application Form
  • Intermediate (HSSC) Certificate and Mark Sheet (original and photocopy)
  • Matriculation (SSC) Certificate and Mark Sheet (original and photocopy)
  • Character Certificate from the last institution attended
  • Copy of Computerized National Identity Card (CNIC) or B-Form
  • Four recent passport-sized photographs with blue background
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Local/Permanent Resident Certificate (PRC)
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Affidavit stating that the student will abide by the rules and regulations of the college

Note: All original documents will be returned after verification. The college reserves the right to verify all documents from the concerned boards/institutions.

Frequently Asked Questions

When do admissions open for the new academic year?

Admissions typically open in July/August for the Fall semester and December/January for the Spring semester. The exact dates are announced on our website and local newspapers.

Can I apply for multiple programs?

Yes, you can apply for multiple programs by indicating your preferences in the application form. However, you will need to meet the eligibility criteria for each program you apply to.

Is there any entrance test for admission?

For most programs, admission is based on academic merit calculated from your Intermediate (HSSC) results. However, for some specialized programs like BS Computer Science, an aptitude test may be conducted.

Are there any reserved seats for special categories?

Yes, there are reserved seats for children of government employees, disabled students, and students from rural areas as per the policy of the Government of Sindh.

Can I pay the fee in installments?

Yes, the college offers a fee installment plan for students who cannot pay the full semester fee at once. You need to apply for this facility at the accounts office with proper documentation.

Is hostel facility available for students?

Yes, the college has a limited hostel facility for outstation students. You need to apply separately for hostel accommodation after securing admission.
